Known Limitations

⚠️

Not Proof of Authenticity

A "LIKELY REAL" result does NOT prove an image is authentic. Only provenance verification can establish origin.

📱

Social Media Filters

Heavy filters (Snapchat, Instagram, TikTok) can cause false positives. The system detects and warns about this.

🎨

Modern Generators

State-of-the-art AI (DALL-E 3, Midjourney v6) may evade detection. Domain generalization is still under validation.

📊

Probabilistic Output

All results are probability estimates with uncertainty. No binary real/fake guarantees are made.
