    Known Limitations

    ⚠️

    Not Proof of Authenticity

    LIKELY REAL does NOT mean authentic. Only provenance verification can establish origin.

    📱

    Social Media Filters

    Heavy filters (Instagram, Snapchat, TikTok) can cause false positives. The system detects and warns about these.

    🎨

    Evolving AI Models

    State-of-the-art generators (DALL-E 3, Midjourney v6+) may evade detection. Results improve as training data grows.

    📊

    Probabilistic Output

    All results are probability estimates. No binary real/fake guarantees are made. Always consider context.

    🎞️

    Video Compression

    Heavy compression artifacts from WhatsApp or Twitter can mimic deepfake artifacts and obscure authentic features.

    ⏱️

    Very Short Clips

    Videos under a few seconds may lack the temporal data required for accurate movement and pulse tracking.

    🏃🏽

    Extreme Motion

    Rapid camera movements, extreme closeups, and heavy strobe lighting can lower frame confidence.

    📊

    Probabilistic Output

    All results are probability estimates. No binary real/fake guarantees are made. Always consider context.

    🔊

    Background Noise

    Heavy background noise, crowd chatter, or overlapping music can interfere with acoustic fingerprinting accuracy.

    📞

    Low Bitrate Audio

    Phone call recordings or extremely compressed files may strip away the neural traits necessary for detection.

    🗣️

    Zero-Shot Cloning

    Top-tier proprietary zero-shot voice cloning may occasionally evade basic acoustic heuristics, though ML catches most.

    ⏳

    Short Clips

    Audio clips under 2 seconds typically lack sufficient phonetic variation for high-confidence predictions.
