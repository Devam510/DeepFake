## What This Service Does

The Synthetic Image Risk Estimator provides **probabilistic analysis** of images to estimate the likelihood that an image exhibits patterns commonly associated with AI-generated content.

This service:
- Analyzes statistical patterns in uploaded images
- Returns a probability estimate with uncertainty
- Provides educational context about limitations
